# circuitracer
circuit racer, 2P game that can be connected towards real life circuit track
--------------------------
CONTROLS: (azerty for now) 
--------------------------
P1:
AZE
QSD  (azerty for now) 

Z: move forward
Q: move backwards
S: move left
D: move right
A: Tilt ball left in minigame
E: Tilt ball right in minigame

--------------------------
P2:
UIO
JKL

I: move forward
K: move backwards
J: move left
L: move right
U: Tilt ball left in minigame
O: Tilt ball right in minigame


Track consist: 
4 Turns
4 checkpoints
2 Switch-Lanes
1 Balance Board (with minigame in the middle)
1 Start/Finish
